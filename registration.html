Last Date of Registration : September 30, 2019

Registration guidelines:

  • Additional papers by the same author will be charged @ 50% of the applicable registration fee per paper for inclusion in the conference.
  • Registration fee for participants includes: Attendance to all the conference sessions, keynotes, conference satchel and materials, certification, souvenir and proceedings, refreshments/meals during the conference, and the conference dinner
  • Registration fee for accompanying person includes: Attendance to all the conference sessions, keynotes, refreshments/meals during the conference.
  • Students should supply valid proof of full-time student status from the University/ Institute to claim student rates.
