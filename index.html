National Conference on Internal Combustion Engines and Combustion

November 1-4 | 2019

National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra | India

About the Conference

The Combustion Institute-Indian Section (CIIS) is pleased to announce and invite you to participate in the 26th National Conference on Internal Combustion Engines and Combustion (NCICEC 2019) in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra during November 1 – 4, 2019. The goal of NCICEC 2019 is to provide a forum for scholars from academia, industry and research institutions to present advances and current challenges in combustion science. It will foster an environment conducive to exchanging ideas and information in the community involved in research and development works in the field of IC Engines, combustion and propulsion.

Combustion scientists are also developing ways to generate electricity from carbon-free fuels such as hydrogen, or from fuels with a low carbon content such as syngas and suppression of ﬁre.

The CIIS

The Combustion Institute-Indian Section (CIIS) is aﬃliated to its parent international body The Combustion Institute situated in Pittsburgh, USA which is a prestigious, non-proﬁt stpr ofessional body, incorporated in the state of Delaware on the July 1 , 1954.

The Combustion Institute (Indian Section) is a professional body registered at Hyderabad. It commenced activities way back in 1974 through a National Symposium in Combustion at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. The same has been continued in the form of a biennial National Conference wherein the engineers and the scientists engaged in the research work in the domain of IC Engines and Combustion are provided a network platform thfor sharing their research contributions. Continuing the series, the 25 NCICEC was held at thNI TK Surthkal during December 15-17, 2017 and 26 NCICEC is proposed to be held at NIT Kurukshetra during November 1-4, 2019. The Combustion Institute: Indian Section oversees the technical and organizational aspects of this and other technical events in the country.
